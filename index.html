My favourite recipe

This is a South African staple that can be enjoyed at any occasion, that along with milktart, malva pudding and some trifle. Below is the recipe for peppermint crisp tart:

  • 1 can of caramel treat
  • 2 cups of whipped cream
  • 1 tub of tennis biscuits
  • 1 bar of peppermint crisp chocolate

Method:

  1. Mix the caramel treat and whipped cream together until smooth.
  2. In a dish, layer tennis biscuits, the caramel mixture, and grated peppermint crisp chocolate. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of the caramel mixture and a generous topping of grated peppermint crisp chocolate.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld and the tart to set.
  4. Serve chilled and enjoy!

My advice

Have fun with it! Next thing you know, you'll be watching Dessert Masters (a spin off from Masterchef Australia) looking for new recipes and techniques, while enjoying something sweet and delicious!

Here are some ideas for your next baking adventure:

  • Try baking a new type of bread, like sourdough or focaccia.
  • Experiment with different types of cakes, such as chiffon or bundt cakes.
  • Explore international desserts, like French macarons or Italian tiramisu.
